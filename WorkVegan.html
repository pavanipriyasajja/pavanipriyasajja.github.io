VeganShare.com - Website Design

Overview of the project:

ABOUT VEGANSHARE.COM WEBSITE: For vegans who want to learn new recipes and share their own with a community of like-minded people, VeganShare is a platform for community members to learn and share healthy and delicious recipes. Unlike allrecipes.com, our product is focused on healthy vegan meals. The goal of our website is people can join as a member, make recipes, share recipes [community forum].

CLIENT: This is one of a Bellevue college UX design course project. This is a 4 Member team project.

METHODS: Brainstorm with business and user, competitive analysis, User Research, User personas, User scenarios, Site map, Sketching, Wireframing, High fidelity of the website , prototyping, User testing.

TIMELINE: 5 Months (Jul 2020 - Nov 2020).

TOOLS: Zoom for communication, Google suite, Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator,pen & paper.

STATUS: Completed

MY ROLE: As a part of a team of (4 members) UX Designers, Conducted user research to find key insights on vegan diet followers, Determine a focus area to design vegan share.com website site architecture diagram to establish the user flow for the website. Performed competitor analysis with other 5 competitor websites to identify strengths and weaknesses of the website. Designed low and Highfidelity mockups with user flow for the vegan share.com website.

DESIGN CHALLENGE: 1. Design and develop the concept of vegan share platform website. 2. Using this platform users can find out millions of vegan recipes and related articles at one place. 3. Establish a community for vegan diet followers where users can post their vegan diet related posts and Events.

User research & Interviews:

1.We found that people usually follow the vegan diet based on their religion, fitness levels, health issues or would like to stay healthy for a long time.

2. Every family has different concerns about their diet like they want to keep their family healthy for aged people or kids or adults.

3. People have less knowledge about the vegan diet and they would like to be more aware about the vegan diet from watching online videos or articles.

4. Users don't find the best recipes on websites, so they don't have much knowledge about vegan recipes.

5. Users might be interested in sharing theri cooking videos through an online website but they don't have many particular vegan websites that they can share recipes.

6. Users would like to have a community website where they can share cooking videos, write articles, participate in recipe contests so users can get more knowledge about veganism.

VeganShare.com is a team project. While working on this project, I have learned the importance of making design decisions on the concept. VeganShare platform is a small website for the vegan people focused. Each designer has a particular perspective on the design. While making design decisions there is a conflict that is include the ecommerce for the vegan platform. Then I have identified the importance of each design decision with the specific reason behind it and this behavior helps me to make correct design decisions on the product. We are focusing on a small website for the vegan food benefits and the recipes. If we include the ecommerce features to this project then it will be a large website of the vegan. This is the most important point that I have learned while working on this project.
